Subjective Time Perception Experiment

Objective

The purpose of this experiment is to measure how different colors influence on time perception

Length

The experiment takes around 10 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Sit 20cm in front of the screen.
  2. Click start and the experiment will enter fullscreen.
  3. Fill short survey.
  4. You will see black screen - this is test. Try to act like durung experiment.
  5. We will try three colors (red, blue, white).
  6. You'll be warned each time color is about to change.
  7. During the experiment click the left mouse button trying to achieve a regular tempo of one click per five seconds.
  8. The colors order will be randomized on each experiment.
