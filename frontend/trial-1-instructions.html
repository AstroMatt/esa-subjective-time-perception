Instructions

  1. Sit 20cm in front of the screen.
  2. Click start and the experiment will enter fullscreen.
  3. Fill short survey.
  4. We will try three colors (red, blue, white) each for 60 seconds.
  5. You'll be warned each time color is about to change.
  6. During the experiment click the left mouse button trying to achieve a regular tempo of one click per five seconds.
  7. The colors order will be randomized on each experiment.
